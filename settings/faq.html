Do you collect/sell my browsing history, location, etc?

We believe in user privacy and so we do not insert ads into our content, sell your data or collect your data, and we never will.

Does this extension exist in other browsers?

Currently the extension is only availible in all Chromium based browsers (Microsoft Edge, Brave, etc).

What permissions do you use, and why do you use them?

We use the following permissions:

"permissions": [ "activeTab", "tabs", "storage" ],
We use these permissions to improve your experience. The "activeTab", "tabs" and "storage" permissions allows us to display the toolbar. The "storage" permission also allows us to save your preferences to your computer so that you don't have to constantly set them.
